Score vs IRMSD Plot
====================

Description:
This plot shows the relationship between docking score and IRMSD.
Each point represents a docked pose (decoy).

Interpretation:
- Lower IRMSD = closer to native structure (better).
- Higher score = better docking prediction.
- Ideal trend: high score at low IRMSD.

Total decoy points plotted: 100

Reference (bound) complexes: 1
  3ZC0: score=64630.98  IRMSD=0.00 (by definition)
